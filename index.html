PromptCrafter

PromptCrafter(tp-cooker) is an innovative software application developed using JavaScript, CSS, and HTML, designed to streamline the process of creating text-to-image prompts. This intuitive web-based tool empowers users to effortlessly generate captivating visual prompts for a variety of applications.

Made with ❤ by tacoen

Features:

  • Working using browser localstorage.
  • Unlimited, Suit You, design your own workspaces, collect your own favorite prompt and mix.
  • The prompts-data is JSON. Can be shared among others.
  • Auto complete shall make you easily remix your prompt.
  • Use suffix and prefix to make your prompting easier.
